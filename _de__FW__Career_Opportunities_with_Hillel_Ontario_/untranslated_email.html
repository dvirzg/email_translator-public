Hillel Ontario is a one-of-a-kind place to build your career - with a hard-working, passionate team, working collaboratively to empower the next generation of Jewish leaders. 

Check out some incredible opportunities below:

 

Director, Hillel TMU


The Director at Hillel Toronto Metropolitan University (TMU) is a creative and engaging individual with a strong Jewish and Israel background, capable of working both independently and as part of a team. You are warm and welcoming, and can bring your passion for building Jewish community to this downtown Toronto campus. You will inspire and mentor students, and will use these skills to engage students and drive Hillel’s mission, values and goals. You will have an entrepreneurial spirit and be flexible and adaptable in building strategies and approaches to an evolving and expanding Hillel.

 

Learn More & Apply

 

Sr. Manager of Community Philanthropy


The Sr. Manager will be an integral part of the Development team, with a primary focus on donors under $5000. The Sr. Manager is a detail-oriented self-starter who will create connections with current and future donors and volunteers. You will manage community fundraising campaigns, utilizing best practices to engage donors via email, direct mail, and personal solicitation. You will also work to support Campus Directors with campus fundraising initiatives.

 

Learn More & Apply

 

Jewski Program Coordinator


Hillel Ontario is looking for a Coordinator to oversee and grow the Jewski program. Our ideal candidate is an experienced, approachable, creative, and dynamic individual who will lead Hillel’s Jewski initiative to engage Russian-speaking students across our nine campuses. You will have the chance to put their mark on initiatives across the organization, from innovation to implementation to evaluation.

 

Learn More & Apply

 

Advocacy Coordinator, Hillel UofT, Hillel York & Hillel Ryerson


The Advocacy Coordinator is an integral part of the local campus team, whose primary focus is on the advocacy initiatives of Hillel’s student leaders. You will be responsible for coordinating and implementing Hillel Ontario’s advocacy strategy on a local campus level and play an integral role in managing, both proactively and reactively, advocacy-related issues on campus. You will cultivate relationships with Jewish and non-Jewish student leadership in pursuit of Hillel’s advocacy objectives.

 

Learn More & Apply
